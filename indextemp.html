About Me



Welcome to my homepage. I am a second year Ph.D. student in Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Texas at Austin, USA. I am associated with Intelligent Data Exploration and Analysis Laboratory (IDEAL) led by Dr. Joydeep Ghosh and Machine Learning Research Group led by Dr. Raymond J. Mooney. My current research interest is in semi-supervised learning, transfer learning and ensemble methods for clustering and classification. Before joining UT Austin, I completed my Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from Jadavpur University, Kolkata, India in year 2009.
